Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as various types of sausage, anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, ham, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Buy dough, pizza sauce, cheese and any toppings you would like.
  2. Spread the dough onto a pizza pan. Make sure you're not making any holes in the dough
  3. Spread the tomato sauce onto the pizza dough evenly.
  4. Sprinkle the cheese onto the pizza evenly.
  5. Fill the pizza with the toppings you chose. Example, onions, tomato, pepperoni, etc.
  6. Put the pizza into the oven and bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown
